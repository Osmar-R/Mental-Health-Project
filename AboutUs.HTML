About Us

Not Just You is a non-profit organization that was founded in 2010 by a group named FAMIO which included five individuals Fatima, Andrew, Itzel, Osmar, and Marisol. Our organization is passionate about bringing awareness to mental health and always working on new ways to help break the stigma on mental illness. Our goal is to help the physical and mental wellbeing of anyone who reaches out to us to ultimately lead to decreased suicide rates in the community.
